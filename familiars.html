Familiars

*All Familiar effects are self-only unless specified.

*Familiars that heal HP and/or MP do so every 10 seconds.

Must-Have Familiars

  • Big Spider - +60% Item Drop Rate and Meso Drop Rate
  • Mutant Ribbon Pig - +60% Item Drop Rate and Meso Drop Rate
  • Mr. Alli - Heals 15% HP and MP
  • Mutant Snail - Heals 15% HP and MP (party effect)
  • Leprechuan - +100% Meso Drop Rate
  • Eye of Time - +100% Meso Drop Rate

Familiars to Use Early On

  • Jr. Boogie - Heals 15% MP
  • Shroom - Heals 5% HP
  • Red Snail - Heals 10% HP on Victoria Island
  • Blue Snail - Heals 10% MP on Victoria Island
  • Octopus - Heals 10% HP and MP on Victoria Island
  • Potted Sprout - Heals 10% HP in Edelstein
  • Potted Morning Glory - Heals 10% MP in Edelstein
  • Water Thief Monster - Heals 10% HP and MP in Edelstein
