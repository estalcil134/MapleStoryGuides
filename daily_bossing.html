Daily Bossing Guide

Zakum

Super simple. Avoid the marks on the floor where the arms will fall.

Kill the arms for EXP, then Zakum itself.

Main Rewards: Eye Accessory, Face Accessory, EXP

Hilla - Normal/Hard

Magnus - Easy/Normal

Ranmaru

You can do both Easy and Madman in one day.

This boss is quite simple. Just watch out for his damage reflect.

Main Rewards: Confusion Fragment(s), Japanese Weapon/Armor Recipes

Arkarium

Main Rewards: Primal Essence(s)

Gollux

Consists of 3 phases

  • Jaw
  • Eyes
  • Gem

Main Rewards: Gollux Belt, Gollux Pendant
