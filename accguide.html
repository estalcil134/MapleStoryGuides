Helpful Tips for Accessory Crafting

Getting Advanced Item Crystals

  • Max Efficiency, Slightly Higher Cost
    1. Gather a bunch of Diamond Ore and refine them
    2. Craft rings using Diamond Gems
    3. Extract the rings (10 at a time if you so please)
  • - This method is guaranteed advanced item crystals.
  • Lower Efficiency, Lower Cost
    1. Gather a bunch of Gold/Topaz Ore and smelt them
    2. Craft rings using Gold Plates or Topaz Gems
    3. Extract the rings (10 at a time if you so please)
  • - This method does not guarantee advanced item crystals as output will include intermediate item crystals.
  • - However, a person is more likely to have a ton of Gold or Topaz on hand as opposed to Diamonds.
  • - Both Gold and Topaz are also easier to obtain and smelt/refine successfully.
  • - Note that this is for those who don't have a lot of mesos; an easier way to obtain is to just buy equips from Leafre.
