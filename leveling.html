Exp Boosters

  • Link Skills
    • Elven Blessing
    • Combo Orb
    • Rune Persistance
  • Buffs
    • Holy Symbol
    • (Beast Tamer skill)
    • Damage Buffs*
      • The more damage you deal, the faster you can kill monsters.
      • The faster you kill monsters, the faster you gain EXP.
    • Kishin Shoukan
      • Not a direct buff, but having one on the map increases spawn rate and max monsters.
      • More monsters means more EXP.
  • Consumables
    • EXP Potion: Alchemy
    • 2x Buffs
      • Ongoing Events
      • Monster Park (Sunday)
    • Monster Park Gold Potion
  • Legion
    • Bonus Character Exp (Zero) *Non-Reboot servers only*
    • Bonus EXP from Legion Board
    • Buff Duration* from Legion Board
      • Self-Used Holy Symbol.
  • Equipment
    • Pendant of the Spirit
      • Monster Park (Sunday)
      • Event Gifts
      • Mu Lung Dojo (Points Shop)
  • Other External Boosters
    • MVP Buff
    • 2x EXP/Drop Rate Events
